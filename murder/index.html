Murder in Phialdelphia

Who Gets Justice?

BY Matthew Albasi

Published July 20, 2018

Philadelphia is a rather murderous city. Over the last 10 years, Philly has had an average of around 300 murders per year. While this many murders hardly gives it the title of 'most murderous city,' the fact of the matter is: roughly 18 out of every 100,000 people will be murdered this year in Philadelphia.

Philly is a racially diverse city. In the 2010 census, Blacks made up more than 43% of the population, Whites 41%, Hispanics 12% and Asians 6%.

Murders by Race

Despite this wide broad range of race, about 78% of all murders in Philadelphia had a black victim.
